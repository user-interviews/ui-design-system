### In Card
When multiple Cards are stacked, you want to ensure there is proper content alignment with padding. When wrapping `Accordion` in `Card`:
1. Use `<Card noPadding>`
2. Use `<AccordionItem borderless>`
2. Use `<AccordionToggle cardPadding>` and `<AccordionCollapse cardPadding>`

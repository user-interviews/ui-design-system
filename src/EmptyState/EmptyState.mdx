## Stories

### Default
- Keep the message and/or icon simple and clear, avoiding jargon or technical terms.
- Use friendly and approachable language to convey that the app is empty.

  <Story id="components-emptystate--default" />

### Illustrations
- Use a visually appealing imagery that matches the style of the rest of the app and catches the user's attention.

### Primary Action
- Offer helpful and actionable tips or instructions to assist the user in populating the area.
- Primary action should usually accept a primary CTA Button.
